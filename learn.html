Frequently Asked Questions

Why is water conservation important?

Water conservation is crucial for preserving our limited freshwater resources, reducing energy consumption, and protecting ecosystems.

How can I reduce water usage in my garden?

Use drought-resistant plants, mulch to retain moisture, water early in the morning or late in the evening, and consider installing a drip irrigation system.

What are some easy ways to save water at home?

Fix leaks promptly, install water-efficient appliances, take shorter showers, and turn off the tap while brushing teeth or shaving.
