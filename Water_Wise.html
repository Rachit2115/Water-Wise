Water Conservation Facts

Understanding the importance of saving water

Why Save Water?

  • The average person uses 80-100 gallons of water per day
  • By 2025, two-thirds of the world's population may face water shortages
  • A running faucet uses about 2 gallons of water per minute
  • A 10-minute shower can use 25 gallons of water
  • Agriculture accounts for 70% of global freshwater usage

Water Saving Tips

  • Fix leaky faucets and toilets
  • Take shorter showers (aim for 5 minutes or less)
  • Turn off the tap while brushing teeth
  • Use water-efficient appliances
  • Collect rainwater for plants
  • Only run full loads of laundry and dishes
